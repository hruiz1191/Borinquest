Game Features

Interactive Exploration

Navigate through vibrant landscapes, ancient ruins, and enchanted environments. Each scene holds hidden layers, secrets, and surprises waiting to be discovered by the curious and the brave.

Branching Dialogues

Engage in meaningful conversations with unique NPCs that remember your actions. Your choices shape their responses, open new paths, and can even unlock special rewards or moral dilemmas.

Inventory & Combinations

Collect magical relics, tools, and mysterious objects throughout your journey. Combine them in clever ways to solve challenges, craft new items, and advance through complex quests and puzzles.

About the Project

Borinquest RPG was born from a deep love for storytelling and the cultural richness of Puerto Rico. It began as an idea to turn traditional narratives and educational principles into an interactive digital experience. Inspired by classic RPGs and the desire to make learning an immersive adventure, Borinquest RPG is both a creative expression and a technical challenge. This project is part of our final portfolio for Holberton School, blending code, art, and culture to create something truly unique.
